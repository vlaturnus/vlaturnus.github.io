title: "Conflicted Analysts and Initial Coin Offerings"
collection: publications
excerpt: 'The study focuses on ICO ratings and demonstrates how conflicts of interest drive affiliated analysts to provide optimistically biased ratings for low-quality projects. Although the ratings, on average, are considered credible, the inflated ratings from conflicted analysts fail to generate higher funding volumes in the short term. Conversely, this discrepancy creates market disagreement, highlighting that less biased ratings possess long-term predictive power for successful ICO ventures.'
